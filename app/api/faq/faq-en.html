FAQ

1/ What is Afrostream ?

Afrostream is a subscription video on demand service available on web browsers, smartphones and tablets which streams the best selection of African, European and African-American movies and TV shows around the Black culture.

2/ How to subscribe to Afrostream ?

You could subscribe directly on the web or your smartphone and tablets.

3/ How long could I subscribe ?

We have two possibilities : you could subscribe on a weekly basis or on a monthly basis

The subscription is without engagement. You could unsubscribe at any time here http://www.afrostream.tv/account

4/ What if I travel in another country ?

Afrostream is available in Great Britain, France, Switzerland, Belgium and all over Africa.

5/ I have a problem. How could I resolve it ?

You should write us here support@afrostream.tv and we will answer in short delays.

6/ I am a content producer, are you interested on ?

Yes ! You should propose your contents here contents@afrostream.tv
